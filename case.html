Executive Summary

GaitGleam, a mid-sized urban shoe store, focuses on offering a wide range of high-quality footwear with a personalized shopping experience. This study highlights the strategies StepAhead implemented to boost growth and customer loyalty.

Background

Founded in 2020 by Daniel Komakech, GaitGleam initially offered limited brands. Today, it features over 50 brands and 300 styles, catering to urban professionals, families, and fashion-conscious individuals.

Objectives

  • Increase sales by 20% in one year.
  • Enhance customer loyalty and retention.
  • Introduce eco-friendly footwear.
  • Improve online presence.

    • Strategies

  • Product Diversification: Added eco-friendly and athleisure footwear.
  • Customer Experience: Trained staff for personalized service and launched a loyalty program.
  • E-Commerce: Developed an online store and leveraged social media.
  • Marketing: Seasonal sales and influencer partnerships.

    • Results

  • Sales Growth: Achieved a 22% increase in sales, with 30% from online.
  • Customer Loyalty: Loyalty program membership up by 40%, with repeat customers contributing 50% of sales.
  • Brand Image: Recognized for sustainable footwear and positive customer feedback.

    • Challenges

  • Supply Chain: Delays addressed by diversifying suppliers.
  • Competition: Mitigated by superior service and exclusive products.

    • Future Plans

  • Expansion: New stores and more sustainable products.
  • Technology: Mobile app and virtual fitting rooms.
  • Community Engagement: Partnering with local schools and organizations.

    • Conclusion

    GaitGleam increased market share and customer base through product diversification, enhanced shopping experience, and improved online presence, positioning itself for continued success.
